What is AndroidDocs?

AndroidDocs is a detailed documentation of the functionality of Android devices. AndroidDocs aims to help new Android users better understand the full power of their device.

Who is AndroidDocs?

AndroidDocs is designed and maintained by Dylan Simowitz.

Why should I care about AndroidDocs?

Improving the usability and knowledge of your device saves time, helps solve future issues, and makes you feel wicked smart.
